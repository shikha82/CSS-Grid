Holiday Destinations: Phi Phi Island (Thailand)

As we all know that Thailand is one of the most promising places for tourists who are willing to go there especially for honeymoon holidays. Phi Phi Island is one of the best holiday destinations in Thailand where people throng from all over the world especially in summer season.

Burma

Burma is a destination perfect for any time throughout the year but January is the perfect time to go to Burma. In January there is lots of sunshine, little rain and a conducive heat. In January its the perfect time that you get to enjoy Burma’s misty mountains, enjoy life in the tropical islands, and learn and experience the historical temples and pagodas across the land.

Australia’s Gold Coast

January starts at a very high note in Australia with Sydney been known for throwing the best parties for the New Year’s Day celebration. In January you get to enjoy the beauty of Australia in amazing places such as Byron Bay to Gold Coast, the Great Barrier Reef, Whitsundays and the east coast. January makes Gold Cost the best destination.
